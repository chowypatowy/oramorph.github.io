College and Olympiad Math Online Network

Links:

  • Main Page
  • Donate
  • Discord
A community where students can gain experience writing math handouts and other students can benefit from these handouts. We have produced college and competition math handouts, AMC mock tests, a collection of problem sets, and more. We have even hosted our own competition, the Winter MathMania, which was sponsored by 6 companies and had $500 in cash prize as well as thousands of dollars in prizes (subscriptions, merch, etc.).
